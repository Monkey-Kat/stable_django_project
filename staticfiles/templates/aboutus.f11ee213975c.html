About

Cleveland Rocks is one part climbing gym and one part community hub. We are dedicated to enhancing both the mind and body through rock climbing, fitness, and technology education.

We're much more than a gym.

Our goal is singular: to create a safe, fun place for active people to find community.

Cleveland Rocks is cultivating an active lifestyle movement that starts with individuals and ends with the community. We're invested in fostering an environment of camaraderie that encourages healthy living and thriving social relationships. We're bringing like-minded individuals under the same roof so that people can meet, interact, and be inspired by one other.
