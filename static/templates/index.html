Built in Ohio City's iconic Franklin Masonic Center, Cleveland Rocks will feature
one of the largest rock climbing gyms in Ohio, a contemporary yoga studio,
and a makerspace (non-profit technology center).

Reach New Heights

Our 18,000 sq. ft. climbing facility will have something for everyone: lead climbing, top rope, and auto-belay walls along with a spacious bouldering area.

For those looking to take their skills to the next level, the training area will feature campus, hang, and moon boards and weight / aerobic rooms.

Find Your Balance

Restore your mind and body through yoga. The studio will be offering group classes in a diverse range of styles from beginner to advanced levels. Our experienced instructors will help you improve your balance, strength, and flexibility.

Engage Your Mind

Flux Makerspace is a technology center committed to making Cleveland a technology hub in the United States. For coding and programming, Flux will offer self-serve resources for those who want to learn at their own pace as well as structured classes for more traditional learning. Flux is committed to fostering a community of innovators in Cleveland.
